Armex introduces you to the BAR Bot

Armex is a passionate developer dedicated to revolutionizing the gaming experience. With a vision to provide gamers with cutting-edge assistance, Armex has created the BAR Bot, a state-of-the-art gaming assistant AI powered by OpenAI. The bot is not officially affiliated to the game Beyond All Reason.

The BAR Bot is designed to elevate your gaming sessions at Beyond All Reason. The bot offers tips, strategies, and personalized recommendations tailored to your unique playstyle.

Armex's commitment to innovation ensures that the BAR Bot stays at the forefront of gaming technology.

Features

  • Real-time player statistics and game data
  • Personalized recommendations tailored to your gaming preferences
  • Seamless integration with a wide range of gaming and communication platforms: Twitch, Discord, ...
  • Active community engagement, allowing interaction with fellow gamers

Twitch Integration

With the Twitch integration, BARBot takes your live streaming experience to the next level. Engage your viewers in exciting ways as they interact with the AI, request real-time information about the game being played and streamed, and receive personalized recommendations tailored to their preferences.

Immerse your audience in captivating gameplay discussions, provide them with valuable insights, and keep them entertained throughout the stream. The Twitch integration with the BAR Bot transforms your live streams into interactive experiences, fostering a strong and engaged community of viewers.

Discord Integration

BARBot is fully integrated with Discord, empowering you to seamlessly interact with the AI directly from your server. Take advantage of real-time game information, and immerse yourself in thrilling gaming discussions with fellow players.

Stay informed about in-game updates, and access a wide range of features designed to enhance your gaming experience. The BAR Bot becomes an invaluable companion, assisting you every step of the way, whether you're strategizing, seeking tips, or engaging in vibrant gaming conversations.

Bot Commands

Command Description
?help Displays the list of available commands.
?whois PlaYer_NAmE Shows the player's statistics of Beyond All Reason.
?ask Ask a question to the BAR Bot AI. The question needs to be related to Beyond All Reason.
?online PlaYer_NAmE Shows online players in the battles context. It counts player in lobby, playing, connecting, spectating. Adding the PlaYer_NAmE show if the online status and location.
?replay PlaYer_NAmE Shows the last player's replay.
?setrating Shows the command to set the rating.
?countask Shows the number of time the command has been typed.
?countwhois Shows the number of time the command has been typed.

Player Age represents the number of matches a player has completed. It serves as an indicator of their experience and progress in the game. The more matches played, the higher the player's age and potential skill level.

Player Skill:

Player Skill indicates the player's current OpenSkill level, reflecting their expertise in the game. It represents the accumulated skill points earned through gameplay, measuring their proficiency. The higher the OpenSkill number, the greater the player's mastery.
